Alexandra House, an Australian private author for the West Coast, is a wordsmith with a penchant for crafting captivating narratives. Hailing from the land down under, Alexandra draws inspiration from the rugged coastlines and vibrant landscapes that surround her. With a gift for storytelling, she weaves tales that transport readers to enchanting worlds filled with intriguing characters and compelling plotlines. Alexandra's Australian heritage infuses her writing with a unique blend of adventure, authenticity, and a deep connection to nature. As a private author catering to the West Coast audience, she brings a fresh perspective and a touch of Aussie charm to her literary creations.
