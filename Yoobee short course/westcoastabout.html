About the Team

West Coast Surf & Street opened their doors in 1999 years in the heart of Dargaville.

Within a few years, West Coast were recognized as the best place in the Kaipara district for street and beach fashion.

Today the store proudly stocks only quality brands from around the world.

West Coast customer service has long been legendary – with friendly and approachable staff, replacement and exchange guarantees.

Come into West Coast 7 days to enjoy the latest fashions from the world’s leading brands.
